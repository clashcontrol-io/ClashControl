## Posting rules

- **Monday:** Start the week with something thought-provoking or an educational how-to. People are in work mode.
- **Tuesday:** Medium format or engagement-bait (poll, question, comparison). Tuesday 8am has the highest LinkedIn B2B reach.
- **Thursday:** Long-form big topic. Thursday 12:30 captures the lunch-break scroll.
- **Friday:** Keep it lighter — feature drops, simple stats, community appreciation. Friday afternoon engagement drops so post by 10am.
- **Skip** public holidays. Never post on a Friday before a long weekend.
- **Re-share** your top performer from the previous month once every 4 weeks with updated framing — LinkedIn's algorithm treats it as new content.

## Image/visual guidance per type

| Post type | Visual |
|-----------|--------|
| Simple | Single high-quality screenshot or clean dark-background graphic with 1-line stat |
| Medium | Screenshot or 2-slide mini-carousel |
| Long | No image OR a personal photo/screenshot — long text posts with images get slightly less reach on LinkedIn; let the copy carry it |
| How-to | 4–6 slide carousel always outperforms static for tutorial content |
| Big topic | No image. Pure text. LinkedIn rewards long-form text-only posts with high dwell time. |
