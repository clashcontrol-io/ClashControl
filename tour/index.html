What is ClashControl?

ClashControl is a free, open-source web app for BIM coordination. Load one or more IFC models, explore them in 3D, and find where elements collide — then track those clashes as issues and round-trip them with the rest of your toolchain through BCF. It does the everyday job of paid tools like Solibri and Navisworks, but runs in a browser tab and costs nothing.

Because everything runs client-side, your model stays on your machine. The full pipeline — viewer, clash engine, issues, BCF, IDS validation and data-quality checks — works without an account and without uploading anything.

Everything it does

Online IFC viewer

IFC 2x3, IFC4 and IFC 4.3 in 3D — orbit, section planes, section box, floor cuts, walk mode, measurement, and a model tree by storey, type, discipline and material.

Clash detection

Hard clashes (geometric intersection) and soft clashes (clearance/tolerance) between disciplines, with rules, grouping and AI-assisted titles and triage.

Visibility clashes

Sight-line / line-of-sight checks — exit-sign visibility, camera coverage, nurse-station observability — a category most tools don't ship.

BCF 2.1 & 3.0

Import and export issues with viewpoints and snapshots, so coordination round-trips with Solibri, BIMcollab, Revit and Navisworks.

IDS validation

Validate models against a buildingSMART IDS specification — author, run and report, all in the browser.

Data quality checks

BIM basics, ILS and NL-SfB classification checks, with a single quality score across the federation.

As-built verification

Align a laser scan (LAS/PLY/PCD/XYZ/PTS/PTX) to the design IFC, then colour the cloud by deviation and turn hotspots into issues.

Live link to Revit

An optional Connector syncs geometry and properties from Revit and pushes detected clashes back — a two-way live link.

Shared projects

Share a project with a short key — no login. Team decisions (status, priority, assignee) sync for everyone.

AI assistant

Plain-language commands, automatic clash titles and triage. Bring your own LLM via a one-click local Connector.

Private by design

Models are parsed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Open-source, so you can read exactly what it does.

Installable PWA

Add it to your machine and use it offline like a native app — same single web app, no separate download.

How it works

1

Load your models. Drop one or more IFC files (or link Revit). They open in 3D in seconds.

2

Run detection. Pick disciplines and a tolerance; ClashControl finds the hard and soft clashes and groups them.

3

Coordinate & export. Review each clash in 3D, assign and prioritise, then export BCF for the rest of your toolchain.

Frequently asked questions

Is ClashControl really free?
Yes — free and open-source (MIT). No license, no subscription, no account.
Does my model get uploaded?
No. IFC parsing and viewing run fully client-side in your browser; your model never has to leave your machine.
Which IFC versions are supported?
IFC 2x3, IFC4 and IFC 4.3 (IFC4X3_ADD2), via the web-ifc WASM engine.
Can it connect to Revit?
Yes — an optional Connector creates a two-way live link, syncing geometry and properties in and pushing clashes back.
How is it different from a plain IFC viewer?
Most free viewers only view. ClashControl adds clash detection, BCF, IDS validation and data-quality checks, so it covers the whole coordination loop. See the comparison of free IFC viewers.
